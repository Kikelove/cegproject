About Us

Centre for Enterprise Governance is a Non-profit Organisation registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission as a Private Company Limited by Guarantee.

The Centre is a Non Government Organisation focused on Enterprise Development through Good Governance.

Vision

Our vision is to build successful entrepreneurs thereby creating socio-economic value for all.

Mission

Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs at all levels to attain success through Multi-Stakeholder dialogue and collaboration for societal value creation.

Obectives of the Centre

  • Educating Entrepreneurs with focus of developing capacity for Good Governance in Business for Sustainable Growth and Success
  • Engaging Entrepreneurs through Advocacy for Corporate Governance and Enterprise Development
  • Creating Socio-economic Value
  • Developing Empowerment Initiatives at various levels of Entrepreneurial Governance
  • Developing competency based research and development in Entrepreneurial Governance
  • Promoting Good Governance through Multi-Stakeholder dialogue in both Private and Public Enterprises
  • Collaborating with organisations for Entrepreneurial success for micro,medium and large Enterprises

Our Core Values

Business Integrity and Commitment to Ethical Value for Enterprise Success

Centre Advisory Board

Meet the Centre for Enterprise Governance Advisory Board that supports and upholds our operations.

Mr. Olu Onakoya (Chairman)

Mr. Olu Onakoya had a varied career involving assignments in Terminal operations,lubricants,plastic container and insecticide manufacturing,corporate planning,building construction,facilities management,human resources, marketing and business leadership. He became the first Nigerian to head the multinational since its inception in 1907. He retired voluntarily in October 2008 after 31 years of service.

He strongly believes in the enormous potential of Nigeria and the capability of its citizens to lift the country to the highest standards of excellence. To him, Nigerians must perform to global standards of ethics and Integrity in business conduct and governance to enjoy respect within the international community.

Experienced and multi skilled Lawyer with a demonstrated history of working in the Financial Services industry. Mrs. Temitope Hassan

Mrs. Temitope Hassan is the Company Secretary/Legal Adviser of Dangote Sugar Refinery Plc. A multi-disciplined Lawyer with over 22 years work experience, she has developed valuable cognitive skills and expertise in Company Secretariat Practice, Legal Drafting, Dispute Resolution, Corporate Governance Advisory, Investor Relations, Compliance and Corporate Services.

She is a certified Trainer and an Accredited Mentor of the National Mentoring Pilot Project, UK. She is a fellow of the institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators of Nigeria.
