Center for Enterprise is a Non-profit Organisation registered with the
Coporate Affairs Commision as a Private company Limited by Gurantee.

The center is a Non Governmental Organisation
focused on Enterprise Development through Good Governance.

Vision

Our vision is to build successful entrepreneurs thereby creating socio-economic value for all.

Mission

Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs at all levels
to attain success through Multi-Stakeholder dialogue
and collaboration for societal value creation.

OUR PROGRAMMES

  • Educating and Engaging Entrepreneurs


  • Empowerment initiatives at various levels of Entrepreneurial Governance


  • Competency based research and development in Entrepreneurial Governance


  • Multi-Stakeholder dialogue in both private and public enterprises


  • Collaborating with organisations for Entrepreneurial success
